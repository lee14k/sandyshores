New Owners

In January, 2022, Sandy Shores Cottages will be run by new owners. Pictured are Keith and Cheryl Steffens welcoming Joe Berube and Susan Murphy to the resort. Joe and Sue are excited about taking over right after the holidays. Past guests will be hearing from them personally about their 2022 reservations. They will also welcome requests for new reservations beginning the first of the year. Joe and Sue can be reached at the same office number used previously, 906-786-3625. Also, guests can reach the new owners through this website, and by the same email as was used by Keith and Cheryl (contact@sandyshorescottages.com) Best of Luck to Joe and Sue. Happy Holidays to all. Be well. Keith and Cheryl Steffens Surrounded by trees and just a few feet from our wide, sandy beach, Sandy Shores Cottages is one of the best places to stay in Northern Michigan. Just minutes from downtown Escanaba and from many attractions, Sandy Shores also offers a calm, peaceful place where you can escape.
